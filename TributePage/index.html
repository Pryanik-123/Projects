Timeline of World War II

Here are the main events of WW2:

  • Jan. 1933 - Hitler becomes German Leader.
  • Sept. 1939 - World War II begins.
  • May 1940 - Hitler invades Denmark and Norway
  • July 1940 - Battle of Britain.
  • Sept. 1940 - Germany, Italy and Japan sign the Tripartite Pact.
  • June 1941 - Hitler attacks Russia.
  • Nov. 1942 - Battle of Stalingrad.
  • Jan. 1944 - German's attempt at taking over Leningrad ends.
  • April 1945 - Hitler commits suicide, Germany loses the war
